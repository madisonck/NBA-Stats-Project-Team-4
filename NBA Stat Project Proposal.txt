NBA Statistics Project

We will be using NBA stats to determine certain tendencies that aren't as apparent and drawing them out. We want to see how different unknown factors influence scoring, winning and advantages/bias. 

NBA project ideas
Best and worst value players?
Free throw percentage bubble vs normal?
Home field advantage bubble vs normal?
Ref tendencies?
All shooting percentages?
Does number of three-point attempts increase playoff chances?
Does a superstar coach allow to players to play better or draw superstar players?
Does coaches career win percentage affect chances of making playoffs?
Left handed shooting vs right handed shooting?
Height variation of players?
